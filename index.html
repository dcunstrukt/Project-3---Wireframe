Compliance Info

In January 2018 the State of California legalized cannabis and imposed new regulations on the cannabis industry. The new regulations require that all cannabis legally sold in California must be water for quality and safety. To do this producers (growers and manufacturers) must have their products tested by a state certified lab before shipping to a retailer.
